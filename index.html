What Every Grade 9 Should Know

    General Tips

  • High School is nothing like High School Musical ;-;
  • High school goes by FAST - try to make the most of it!
  • Set goals for life.
  • Have fun and enjoy high school. Make memories and make friends.
  • Be fearless.
  • Share your opinions.
  • It's okay to say no.
  • It's okay for you to say that things are hard, just remember that you're not alone, and that there are so many people who are willing to help.
  • Setbacks mean there is room to improve.
  • Take breaks.
  • Sometimes there are things more important than school: your health, your relationships, and your interests.
  • Do whatever you can for yourself and others, without expecting anything back. It'll make you and everyone else happy ^_^
  • Eat full meals every day.
  • If you have to buy lunch, go to South Caf ;)
  • Champions aren't born overnight.

    • Work

  • Don't be afraid to join clubs and try new things. You will learn so much about yourself, and make many new friends.
  • Don't procrastinate, prioritize your work.
  • Have an organized schedule. You'll have many responsibilities, but planning will help manage them.
  • Work hard in school, because it's always worth it.
  • Study for tests and quizzes - high school matters.
  • Don't bite off more than you can chew.
  • Listen to your teachers.
  • Skipping or coming late isn't cool - you're gonna suffer in the end.

    • Mental Health

  • Your mental health is important and it matters!
  • Don't think that you have to be the best at something or everything to be recognized. You will be loved no matter what <3
  • Have self worth. It's easy to compare yourself with other people, but you need to be confident and understand that you're capable!
  • Always believe in yourself! You're braver and smarter than you will ever know!
  • Find balance between going out and doing your school work.
  • Instagram followers don't mean anything!
  • Love yourself. You're amazing <3
  • Understand that everyone has different goals. Don't get discouraged when people around you are achieving their goals faster than you.
  • High school is the time you learn. Don't get upset from a few failures.

    • Individuality

  • Don't try and fit in, just do you.
  • Don't be afraid to express yourself how you want.
  • Try to face your fears and challenges instead of letting them hold you back.
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes.
  • Trust yourself.

    • Relationships

  • Maintain good healthy relationships with your family - be honest with them.
  • You are influenced most by the 5 closest people to you; choose those 5 people carefully.
  • Teachers are not out to get you!
  • If you're feeling down, there's always someone to talk to.
  • Be open to making new friends.
  • Talk! Talking and communicating is important and tons of fun!
  • Get rid of toxic people.
  • Don't be hesitant to ask for help. Everyone wants the best for you but you have to ASK.
  • Avoid drama at all costs, it's not going to do you good.
  • Don't judge.
